Renu Khator

President of the University of Houston

Chancellor of the University of Houston system

Interesting career facts about Dr Khator

  • The first Indian American to lead a major research university in the United States
  • 1973 - Bachelor's degree from Kanpur Univeristy
  • 1985 - PhD Political Science, Purdue University
  • 1985 - 1987 - University of South Florida, rose to senior Vice President
  • 2007 - Dual position as Chancellor of the University of Houston System and president of the University of Houston
  • 2011 - Appointed to the board of directors at the Federal Reserve Bank of Dallas
